About Dr. Martello

Dr. Martello has been proudly serving the Baton Rouge community as a dedicated chiropractor for many years, helping patients find long-term relief from pain and discomfort. With extensive training in spinal health and musculoskeletal care, he focuses on treating the root cause of pain.

His patient-first approach ensures every individual receives affordable personalized care designed to restore mobility, reduce pain, and improve overall quality of life.

At Martello Chiropractic, the mission is simple: help you feel better, move better, and live better.
